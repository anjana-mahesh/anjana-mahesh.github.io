I am currently a Ph.D. student (thesis submitted) at the Dept. Of Electrical Communication Engineering, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. I received my B. Tech in Electronics and Communication from Kerala University, Trivandrum, and my M.E. in Telecommunications (gold medalist) from the Dept. Of ECE, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore.

Prior to joining the Ph.D. program, I had a brief stint in the industry. I held positions at Qualcomm India Private Limited and Lekha Wireless Solutions, where I primarily worked on 4G and 5G modem systems.

My research interests are in the broad area of information and coding theory. The work towards my Ph.D. thesis focused on index coding, which is a variant of source coding for broadcasting to receivers with side information, and its applications in coded caching, D2D communication, and coded distributed computing. My research was partly funded by the Qualcomm Innovation Fellowship awarded to me in 2020.
